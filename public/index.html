About The Page

Students often face problem in searching for resources best suited for their studies. Most of them are paid and the free ones need to be searched throughout internet taking away their precious time of study. So, here at [Re]Sources we are providing SCIT students with a platform to cater to all their study material needs so that they can study engineering on their own.
The interface is designed to be easy to use and is the one stop place for all of our studying needs.
We have only one aim: Education must be free and available to all.
